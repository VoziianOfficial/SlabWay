Privacy Overview

SlabWay may collect information that users voluntarily submit through website forms, request paths, or contact links. This information helps organize concrete project requests and supports the process of comparing local provider options.

Platform Role

SlabWay is an independent concrete service matching platform. The website helps users describe concrete project needs and review concrete service categories such as driveways, patios, slabs, repairs, stamped concrete, sidewalks, and walkways.

SlabWay does not directly pour concrete, install concrete, repair concrete, inspect concrete, guarantee provider work, or perform concrete services.

Information We May Collect

When you use the contact form or start a concrete request, you may provide information such as:

  • Full name and contact details.
  • Email address and phone number.
  • Concrete service category selected in the form.
  • Project details, surface notes, timeline, size, location, or preferences.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type, approximate device data, or website interaction details.

How Information May Be Used

Submitted information may be used to respond to inquiries, organize concrete request details, improve website functionality, maintain security, and support the provider matching process.

SlabWay may also use non-sensitive website interaction information to improve page performance, content clarity, form usability, and overall user experience.

Sharing Information

SlabWay may share submitted request information when reasonably necessary to support provider-matching or respond to user inquiries. Information may also be shared when required by law, to protect rights, or to maintain website security.

SlabWay does not sell itself as a direct concrete contractor and does not claim to personally perform concrete services.

Data Security

SlabWay uses reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, email, hosting system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

User Choices

Users may choose not to submit a request form. Users may also contact SlabWay using the email listed on this website to ask questions about submitted information.

Browser settings may allow users to limit cookies, block certain storage tools, or clear local storage. Some website features may not work as intended if browser storage is disabled.
