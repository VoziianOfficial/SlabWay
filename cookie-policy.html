Cookie Overview

Cookies and local storage help the SlabWay website remember basic preferences, support website functionality, and improve the browsing experience while users review concrete service categories or start a concrete request.

Platform Role

SlabWay is an independent concrete service matching platform. The website helps users review concrete service paths and compare local provider options.

SlabWay does not directly pour, install, repair, inspect, guarantee, or perform concrete work.

What Cookies And Storage May Do

SlabWay may use cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, or similar browser technologies for purposes such as:

  • Remembering cookie consent choices.
  • Supporting website navigation and interactive features.
  • Improving page usability and performance.
  • Understanding general website interaction patterns.
  • Maintaining basic security and form experience.
Type Purpose Example
Essential Supports core website functionality. Navigation, form behavior, browser compatibility.
Preference Remembers user choices. Cookie banner accept or decline choice.
Performance Helps understand how the website performs. General interaction or page experience signals.

Cookie Consent Banner

SlabWay includes a cookie consent banner that allows users to accept or decline non-essential cookie use. The selected choice may be stored in localStorage so the banner does not keep appearing after a decision is made.

Clearing browser storage may reset the banner and require the user to choose again.

Accept

Saves the consent choice and allows the website to remember the preference.

Decline

Saves the decline choice and keeps the website from repeatedly showing the banner.

Third-Party Libraries

The SlabWay website may load front-end libraries from content delivery networks for icons, animations, or sliders. These libraries help the website display interactive design features such as Lucide icons, AOS animations, and Swiper sliders.

Third-party services may process limited technical information such as browser request data when files are loaded from their servers.

User Choices

Users can adjust cookie settings through the cookie banner when it appears. Users may also clear cookies, localStorage, and browser cache through browser settings.

Blocking or clearing browser storage may affect some website preferences or interactive features.
